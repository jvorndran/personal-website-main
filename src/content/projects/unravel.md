Engineered a platform for rapid RAG proof-of-concept deployment and inspection, with layout-aware document parsing through Docling, vector embedding, and hybrid retrieval.

The retrieval workflow uses LLM-driven query variations, Reciprocal Rank Fusion, and cross-encoder reranking to maximize context precision.
